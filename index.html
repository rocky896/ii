Poll Box

Your Opinion Matters.

Poll Box is an app for the people of world to express their opinions and views. This will revolutionize the way we share or reviews or opinons about products and services aroundt he world. We at Indovations Inc believe in Democracy and each individual has a say about what they want. With Poll Box we would like to minimize the polls to minimum options and as a customer we would like to place the power in your hands about a company's product or a service. Poll Box can be extended to research and ad-hoc polls. No more hand raising to get rough estimate. with pollbox your opinions and choices are instant. The companies can also use your polls to make a quick decision on if the public has liked their new product or not.
